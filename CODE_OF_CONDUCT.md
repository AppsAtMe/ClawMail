# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in ClawMail a welcoming experience for everyone — humans and agents alike.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Welcome newcomers
- Accept feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [apps@andrewrmitchell.com](mailto:apps@andrewrmitchell.com). All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
